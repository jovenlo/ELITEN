About Eliten

Welcome to Eliten, where timeless elegance meets modern craftsmanship


Founded in 1912 by master jeweler Edgar LeClair, Eliten began as a small, family-run atelier nestled in the heart of Paris. With a passion for creating exquisite, handcrafted pieces, Edgar’s vision was to bring luxury and artistry to every design. His talent quickly attracted the attention of Europe’s elite, and Eliten became synonymous with elegance and exclusivity.

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Over the past century, Eliten has upheld Edgar’s commitment to exceptional quality. Each piece we create is a tribute to the precision and care that define the Eliten tradition. Our jewelry is designed by skilled artisans who pour their creativity and expertise into every detail, ensuring that every gemstone is perfectly set, and every precious metal gleams with brilliance.

Modern Elegance, Timeless Beauty

While Eliten treasures its heritage, we are always evolving. Today, we blend traditional techniques with innovative designs to offer a range of jewelry that speaks to the modern sophisticate. From stunning engagement rings to bespoke necklaces, our collections are crafted to celebrate life’s most important moments.

A Trusted Name in Luxury

Eliten has adorned royals, celebrities, and collectors across the globe. Our pieces have been featured in prestigious events and worn by those who appreciate the finer things in life. When you choose Eliten, you are not just wearing jewelry — you are becoming part of a rich history of beauty, grace, and luxury.

Our Promise

At Eliten, we believe that jewelry should be a reflection of your unique story. That’s why we work closely with our clients to create pieces that are as individual as they are. We pride ourselves on providing impeccable service, ensuring that every Eliten customer experiences the same care and attention to detail that Edgar LeClair envisioned over a century ago.

Join Us

We invite you to explore our collection and discover why Eliten has remained a trusted name in jewelry for over 100 years. Whether you are searching for a timeless piece or a modern masterpiece, you will find it at Eliten.
